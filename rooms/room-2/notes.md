Room 2 Notes — Blue Team & SOC
Blue Team / SOC
Protects systems by detecting and stopping intrusions.


SOC (Security Operations Center) monitors networks and systems for suspicious activity.


SOC Responsibilities
Trend & Vulnerability Awareness – Stay updated on threats and vulnerabilities.


Policy Violations – Make sure company rules are followed.


Unauthorized / Illegal Activity – Watch for activity outside the normal baseline.


Intrusion & Breach Detection – Detect and respond to breaches quickly.


Digital Forensics
Investigates digital devices to preserve and analyze evidence.


Can examine:


File Systems – Installed programs, created/deleted/modified files.


Memory – Malicious programs running in memory.


System Logs – Activity on systems.


Network Logs – Network traffic to track attacks.


Incident Response (IR) Process
Preparation – Set up teams, infrastructure, and preventive measures like phishing training.


Detection & Analysis – Use tools and logs to detect incidents and measure impact.


Containment, Eradication & Recovery – Stop the attack, remove threats, restore systems.


Post-Incident Activity – Review what happened, learn lessons, and improve policies or training.
